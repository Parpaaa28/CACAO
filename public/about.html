Hi, We're CACAO CENTRAL

We craft cacao essentials from bean to bar, celebrating Philippine origins with modern, minimalist design.

About the Brand

CACAO CENTRAL is a homegrown cacao shop focused on small-batch quality, traceable sourcing, and everyday rituals. We partner with local farmers and makers to bring you beans, bars, and craft essentials that feel refined yet approachable.

From raw cacao and cocoa powders to tools and gift sets, every item is curated for flavor, function, and thoughtful gifting.

Mission

To celebrate Philippine cacao by making high-quality, responsibly sourced cacao essentials accessible to everyday rituals and gifting.

Vision

To be the most trusted cacao destination in the country, connecting farmers, makers, and modern cacao lovers through thoughtful design and craft.

Our Craft

We focus on small-batch roasting, careful fermentation profiles, and flavor-forward recipes that highlight origin.

Farm Partners

We collaborate with local farmers and co-ops for traceable beans, fair pricing, and long-term community support.

Design Ethos

Minimal, functional packaging that keeps cacao fresh and looks clean on shelves and gift sets.
